Take a guided tour through the molecular machines of a bacterial cell or explore on your own. Zoom from a whole cell down to its atoms. Available on iPad, iPhone, and Apple Vision Pro.

[Download on the App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/cellwalk/id1627776848) – [CellWalk.ca](https://cellwalk.ca) – [Follow me on X](https://x.com/timd_ca)

Created by Timothy Davison and HK. Model and tour by [David Goodsell](https://x.com/dsgoodsell), [Ludovic Autin](https://x.com/ludovic_autin), and [Martina Maritan](https://x.com/MartinaMaritan), built on their [2022 whole-cell paper](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jmb.2021.167351) with Jonathan Karr, Markus W. Covert, and Arthur J. Olson.

Thank you to the many people who have helped support CellWalk along the way.
