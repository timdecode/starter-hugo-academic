CellWalk was named a finalist in the Visuals and Graphics category of the [2025 Apple Design Awards](https://www.apple.com/newsroom/2025/06/apple-unveils-winners-and-finalists-of-the-2025-apple-design-awards/), announced ahead of WWDC25. Apple describes the category as recognizing apps with "stunning imagery, skillfully drawn interfaces, and high-quality animations that elevate the experience."
