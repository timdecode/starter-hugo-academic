I spoke at [KWDC25](https://kwdc.dev/) — the Koreawide Developer Conference — at the aT Center in Seoul, sharing the design principles behind CellWalk for Apple Vision Pro. The talk, *Thoughtful Spatial Design: Exploring Biology from Cells to Atoms*, walked through how we make a whole bacterial cell legible and navigable in spatial computing, from continuous scale transitions down to atoms to the interaction patterns that keep learners oriented inside a crowded molecular environment.
