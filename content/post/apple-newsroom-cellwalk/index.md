Apple featured [CellWalk](https://cellwalk.ca) in a [newsroom article](https://www.apple.com/ca/newsroom/2025/09/apples-foundation-models-framework-unlocks-new-intelligent-app-experiences/) on apps built with the Foundation Models framework introduced in iOS 26, iPadOS 26, and macOS 26. The piece describes CellWalk as "the immersive biology app" that "lets users explore a detailed 3D cell down to the molecular level, or take a tour through life's molecular machines."
